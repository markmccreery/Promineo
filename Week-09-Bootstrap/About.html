About Us

We were founded in September 2012 in Las Vegas, Nevada between two friends who wanted to provide music to k-12 students. Currently today we have nine faculty members who provide musical training in voice, piano, guitar, strings, and percussion. Originally we didn't have an office space and had to teach lessons by administering them outside of our houses. However, we now have a office space located on 4000 E Harmon Road, Las Vegas, NV, 89121.

We at Code9 are always striving to provide music to underpriviledged communities and children in order to provide music education to those in need. We want to provide music education to all k-12 students in the Greater Las Vegas Area. We are also dedicated to providing clinics and masterclasses throughout the United States.

We are always striving to make strong musicians out of all of our students! Currently we have about 500 students enrolled in the music program and we are striving to double that by 2025. Code9 is also dedicated to providing all students enrolled in our programs assistance when trying to access higher education in music whether through grants or instrument loans.
